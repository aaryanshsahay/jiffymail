Purpose:

The ability to send a large number of mails to a fixed amount of recivers made simple , easy fast and convinient. With the help of this , say goodbye to spending hours configuring a mail to hundreds of people. Perfect for teachers, HR , managers and other similiar occupations.

Instructions:

1)Enable less secure apps from your gmail account for this to work(you can turn it back off after performing the task): Click here

3)Future updates: Ability to include various file formats (jpg , pdf ,etc) to be added.
